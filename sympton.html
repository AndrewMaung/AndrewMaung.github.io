COVID-19 Symptoms

COVID-19 affects people in different ways. Some people may be asymptomatic, while others may experience severe symptoms. Understanding the symptoms can help in early detection and treatment.

Here are some common symptoms associated with COVID-19:

Fever

A high temperature is one of the most common symptoms of COVID-19.

Cough

A persistent cough that may be dry or productive is a key symptom.

Fatigue

Feeling unusually tired or lethargic can be a symptom of infection.

Loss of Taste or Smell

A sudden loss of taste or smell is a strong indicator of COVID-19.

Difficulty Breathing

Trouble breathing or shortness of breath can indicate severe symptoms.

Sore Throat

A painful throat can be another sign of COVID-19 infection.

Chills

Chills or shivering may occur along with fever and other symptoms.

Headache

Frequent headaches can be a common symptom associated with COVID-19.

Muscle Pain

Muscle or joint pain can be a sign of COVID-19 infection.
